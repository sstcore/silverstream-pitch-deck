Trade Finance Division

What is Trade Finance?

It bridges the gap between when a seller ships goods and when a buyer pays. It funds ~80-90% of global trade.

Letter of Credit (L/C)

Bank guarantees payment to exporter once shipping docs verified. Most common instrument in commodity trade.

Invoice Financing

Seller gets cash advance against an issued invoice. Platform buys the receivable at a discount. Typical tenor: 30-90 days.

SilverStream will focus on Invoice Financing & L/C-based deal flow — highest volume, clearest documentation trail.

The Core Concept: Collateral.

Think of it like a house mortgage.

The House Analogy

If you own a $2M house, you can tell the bank:
"Hold my house as security and lend me $1M."

You still own the house, but the bank has a legal claim if you don't repay.

Here, instead of a house, it's Bitcoin.

What does Sygnum do?

Sygnum is not just an escrow. They are the lender and custodian.

  • 1. Holds the Investor's $1,000,000 BTC as collateral.
  • 2. Lends $500,000 cash to the Exporter.
  • 3. Receives repayment 60 days later and releases the BTC.

Preserving Upside: Why Pledge?

For long-term holders who expect BTC to appreciate but want to generate yield today.

The 50% LTV Cushion

Sygnum only lends $500k against $1M in BTC. This conservative 50% Loan-to-Value (LTV) gives the ecosystem a massive safety cushion against standard crypto volatility.

Holding vs. Pledging

If you hold, your yield is 0%. By pledging, you retain 100% of the underlying Bitcoin upside while earning an attractive, real-world yield from the trade finance loop.

Automated Risk Management

What happens during extreme market volatility? The institutional custodian manages risk algorithmically.

The Margin Call

If BTC drops significantly (approaching ~65% LTV), Sygnum issues a margin call: "Deposit more BTC/cash." If the investor doesn't, Sygnum automatically liquidates just enough BTC to cover the risk. The loan is always protected, and the investor's remaining BTC is preserved.

Why does anyone do this?

Understanding the exact incentives for every participant.

Why doesn't Exporter use own collateral?

Many exporters don't have $1M in liquid real estate, don't want to tie up assets, or need financing faster than a local bank can provide. Trade finance relies on the cargo and invoices, not just real estate.

Why does Sygnum agree?

Because Sygnum earns interest. On a $500k loan, they might earn $10,000. They generate revenue while being 100% protected by highly liquid overcollateralized Bitcoin.

Why Investor helps a stranger?

They aren't "helping". It's an investment—like buying a corporate bond. The investor says: "I'll allow my BTC to be used as collateral if I receive an attractive yield while still capturing BTC's upside."
