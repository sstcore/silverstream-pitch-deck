Trade Finance Division

What is Trade Finance?

It bridges the gap between when a seller ships goods and when a buyer pays. It funds ~80-90% of global trade.

Letter of Credit (L/C)

Bank guarantees payment to exporter once shipping docs verified. Most common instrument in commodity trade.

Invoice Financing

Seller gets cash advance against an issued invoice. Platform buys the receivable at a discount. Typical tenor: 30-90 days.

SilverStream will focus on Invoice Financing & L/C-based deal flow — highest volume, clearest documentation trail.
